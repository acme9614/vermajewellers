The foundation of “VERMA JEWELLERS, SOLAN” was laid by the late Shri Jawala Prasad Verma in 1967. He was joined by his eldest son Shri Naresh Chand Verma and the father-son team set their foot to work hard and establish Verma Jewellers in the heart of Solan town. During the next twenty-five years, both of them, with a positive attitude, took certain concrete steps to expand. They included the adjacent area into a larger shop, the present shop in 1995.

Mr. Naresh Chand Verma was joined by his young and dynamic son Akshay Verma in 2002 after completing his studies in Gemology, Diamond Grading, and Jewellery Manufacturing from the Indian Diamond Institute, Surat.

The first big step of Akshay Verma was to get Hallmarked Certification from the Bureau of Indian Standards for 100% Hallmarked Jewellery. They have the honour to be the first jewellery store certified by BIS in Himachal Pradesh in September 2002. Leveraging his strengths in business development, product development, human resources, and administration, Akshay Verma has been the catalyst of growth for the brand.

A new-age professional, well-versed with the requirements and challenges of a globalised work environment, Akshay Verma is the powerhouse behind the stupendous success of one of Himachal Pradesh’s most renowned jewellery brands – Verma Jewellers.

From the day he joined the business in 2000 at age 21, he relied on passion and vision to build a towering brand. What began as a 700 sq.ft shop with 5 employees has grown into a 3500 sq.ft showroom with over 60 employees.

Today, Verma Jewellers boasts a vast variety of Gold, Diamond & Silver jewellery meticulously sourced from Delhi, Kolkata, Mumbai, Surat, and Ahmedabad. Their exclusive Diamond Jewellery, specially handcrafted by experts, is unique and exceptional.
